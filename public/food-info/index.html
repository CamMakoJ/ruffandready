Apple

Information:

feed your pet apples in moderation and remove the core and seeds first as they contain cyanide

Benefits:

High in fibre

Low in fat

Helps maintain Healthy bones and tissue

Ideal snack for overweight or senior pets who may have lower metabolism

Nutrients:

Vitamins A & C
