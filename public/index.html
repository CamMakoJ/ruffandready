4 lb case | 12 lb case | 24 lb case

55% Beef trim, 35-40% organ meats (Heart, Kidney & Liver), 5-7% sterilized pulverized eggshell* (Natural source of calcium & other nutrients)

Serving Suggesions:

Looking for serving suggestions? Check out our info on Fruit & Vegetables including serving suggestions.For example, try adding 1/2 oz of kale and 1/2 oz of carrot for a dog eating a 4 oz Ruff’n’Ready raw Beef patty.
